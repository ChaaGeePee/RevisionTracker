✅ Week 4 Checklist

Revision Criteria by Subject

📖 AQA English Language

  • Paper 1: List 4 things, language analysis, structural features, evaluation, creative writing
  • Paper 2: True/False, summary, language analysis, compare perspectives, non-fiction writing
  • Key skills: Inference, language analysis, structural analysis, evaluation, planning

📚 AQA English Literature

  • Macbeth: ambition, guilt, power, appearance vs reality, fate & free will
  • A Christmas Carol: redemption, poverty, social responsibility, greed, transformation
  • An Inspector Calls: responsibility, class, gender, capitalism vs socialism
  • Poetry: Worlds and Lives Anthology – compare named poems with unseen

🔬 AQA Biology

  • Paper 1: Cell biology, organisation, infection & response, bioenergetics
  • Paper 2: Homeostasis, inheritance, variation, ecology
  • Key skills: Required practicals, 6-mark extended answers, data interpretation

⚗️ AQA Chemistry

  • Paper 1: Atomic structure, bonding, quantitative, extraction, chemical changes, energy
  • Paper 2: Rate & equilibrium, organic, analysis, atmosphere, using resources
  • Key skills: Moles calculations, titrations, yield, empirical formula

⚡ AQA Physics

  • Paper 1: Energy, electricity, particle model, atomic structure
  • Paper 2: Forces, waves, magnetism, space (separate only)
  • Key: Memorise all required equations listed in specification

🌍 AQA Geography

  • Paper 1: Natural hazards, living world, physical landscapes
  • Paper 2: Urban issues, economic world, resource management
  • Paper 3: Issue evaluation (pre-released), fieldwork
  • Key skills: OS maps, graphs, data interpretation, 9-mark extended answers

🇫🇷 AQA French

  • Paper 1: Listening (25%) – audio extracts, multiple choice, gap fill
  • Paper 2: Reading (25%) – vocabulary, translation French→English
  • Paper 3: Speaking (25%) – role play, photo card, general conversation
  • Paper 4: Writing (25%) – message, structured question, translation English→French

🎭 OCR Drama

  • Component 1: Devising Drama (40%) – create, perform, portfolio
  • Component 2: Performance from Text (20%) – two scripted extracts
  • Component 3: Theatre Makers in Practice (40%) – live theatre analysis, set text interpretation
  • Key concepts: Stanislavski, Brecht, staging types, proxemics, status

🔢 Edexcel Maths

  • Paper 1: Non-Calculator (33%)
  • Papers 2 & 3: Calculator (33% each)
  • Topics: Number, algebra, graphs, ratio, geometry, probability, statistics
  • Higher only: vectors, calculus, advanced trig, proof

📜 Edexcel History

  • Paper 1: Medicine in Britain c.1250–present + Western Front (30%)
  • Paper 2: Cold War 1941–1991 + Early Elizabethan England 1558–1588 (40%)
  • Paper 3: The USA 1954–1975 (30%)
  • Key skills: Source analysis (utility, reliability, provenance), narrative accounts, essays

Revision Tips & Strategies

🧠 Active Recall

Instead of re-reading notes, test yourself using flashcards, past paper questions, or self-quizzing. This is the most effective revision technique.

🔁 Spaced Repetition

Revisit topics after 1 day, 3 days, then a week. This forces your brain to strengthen memory pathways over time.

📝 Past Papers (Sundays)

Complete every Sunday paper under strict timed, exam conditions — no notes, no phone. Mark immediately using official mark schemes and log your score.

🍅 Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes then take a 5-minute break. After 4 sessions take a 20-minute break. Maintains focus throughout weekday sessions.

📋 Exam Command Words

Know exactly what "describe", "explain", "evaluate", "analyse", "compare", and "assess" mean in each of your specific subjects.

💤 Look After Yourself

Sleep 8–9 hours. Exercise regularly. Eat well. Your brain consolidates memory during sleep — revision without sleep is significantly less effective.
